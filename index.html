United We Overcome

About us

United Hearts is a non-profit organisation that aims to raise awareness of the number of orphans in Jakarta who are not getting the education they need, and to help them receive the education they deserve. Through exciting lessons, we hope we will be able to increase their knowledge and help improve the quality of education in underprivileged parts of Jakarta.

Sembako

Week 2: On 22 May 2020 , we delivered another 100 packets of sembako to the people around Joglo.

Week 3: On the 19th of June, we delivered 100 packets of sembako to Petamburan, Grogol. We are so glad that we are able to help more and more people ease through this pandemic. Teaching Session

United Hearts has been planning for this collaboration with Yayasan Prima Unggul. YPU is a place where children get to show their talents and build their characters. The children are not as privileged as us. However, they are still very hardworking and thankful for the chances they get to further develop their skills and talents. United Hearts decided to help teach the children in YPU how to communicate in English. We are so happy to be able to share our knowledge to people around us in this season of giving.
